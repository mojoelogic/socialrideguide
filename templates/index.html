SRG Podcast

Circle round and listen close for precious tips from the lips of those who have created and sustained pedal powered communities.

This is not your typical two wheeling podcast. Our focus is on finding what worked, and just as importantly what hasn't worked, from the organizers of beloved social cycling event communities.

This episode features the organizer of History Bike Gainesville, which is quickly approaching a decade of rides. Don’t let the brevity of this episode deceive you. There are many gems and takeaways for organizers who want to build rides that outlast them.

Nigel Rudolph of History Bike Gainesville recorded at Depot Park in Gainesville, FL.

Follow HistoryBike on Instagram.

This episode is part of the Ask The Organizers series and features 3 of 4 organizers for CoffeeOutside Gainesville, which just celebrated its 2 year anniversary. As you’ll hear, the friendship and interplay of the co-organizers led to equal parts laughter and actionable insights all the way to the end.

Ana, Seth, and Logan of CoffeeOutside Gainesville recorded at Depot Park in Gainesville, FL.

Follow CoffeeOutside.GNV on Instagram.
